## IX Framework — Intelligent Experiences

URL: https://affordance.design/ix.html

AI without UX is cold automation. UX without AI is static and slow. IX unites them to create adaptive systems that serve human intent first. The IX Framework manages the complexity of systems that think, adapt, and respond.

### The Five Lenses

1. **Intent** — Begin with clarity of purpose. Intent shapes outcomes and should start with empathy, not efficiency. AI fails not because of bad technology but because of unclear intent.

2. **Interpretation** — Translate data into meaning with transparency. Show how inputs map to inferences so people can steer, correct, and trust the system. Includes reason previews and correction UI.

3. **Interaction** — Treat the interface as a dialogue. Adapt tone, pace, and modality to context and emotion. Make trust moves visible at key moments. Includes tone systems and adaptive states.

4. **Inference & Iteration** — Shape how the system learns and keep learning aimed at the right thing. Build feedback loops, fairness checks, and human-in-the-loop pathways. Includes bias audits and iteration guardrails.

5. **Impact** — Ask who benefits, who is burdened, and who might be left out. Measure human value: trust, inclusion, empowerment, and wellbeing — not just clicks. Includes who/whom analysis and redress plans.

### Applied Model (5 Phases)

1. Discovery — Define human intent, contextual interviews, emotional mapping. Outputs: Intent Map, Goal-state Journey, AI Opportunity Matrix.
2. Data & Context — Map signals, signal-to-intent mapping, bias audits. Outputs: Signal Map, Context Layers Canvas, Data Ethics Charter.
3. Dialogue Design — Interaction flows, tone systems, adaptive states. Outputs: Dialogue Flows, System Personality Sheet, Adaptive State Map.
4. Learning Systems — Feedback loop architecture, human-in-the-loop oversight. Outputs: Feedback Loop Blueprint, Trust Moments Map, Bias Mitigation Plan.
5. Impact — Measure trust, adaptivity metrics, fairness checks. Outputs: IX Impact Dashboard, Human Value Metrics, Inclusion Heatmap.

### IX Artefacts

- **IX Canvas** — One-page blueprint to define motivations, signals, dialogue, feedback, and impact.
- **Personality Sheet** — Defines voice, tone shifts, vocabulary, and safety rails for AI systems.
- **Feedback Blueprint** — How users teach the model. Routes for retraining and oversight.

### Application Domains
- Education: Adaptive learning that adjusts pace and celebrates effort.
- Healthcare: Companion systems that shift tone on detected stress with transparency indicators.
- Recruitment: Bias-aware screening with fairness visualizations and human-in-the-loop retraining.
- Retail: Contextual commerce that explains recommendations and promotes mindful purchasing.
