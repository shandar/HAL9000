## Products & Creations

AI-powered timeline intelligence engine. A deterministic, hallucination-resistant timeline intelligence engine. Generates interactive chronological timelines with 2D visualization and immersive 3D experience. 100+ users and 80 paid users in just 1 month of launch. Credits-based usage system.
- Status: Live
- URL: https://timelinex.in

### CTRL+Survive
A darkly humorous career survival runner simulating modern working life. Navigate 300 seconds across 4 stages: Education, Corporate, Layoff, and the AI Era. Balance Relevance, Burnout, Savings, and Adaptability. 100+ users in the first week of launch.
- Status: Live
- URL: https://affordance.design/ctrl+survive
- Tech: Claude Code, PhaserJS, TypeScript

### Project Workflow Protocol
An open-source set of Skills for Claude Code that standardize how software projects are planned, built, verified, and shipped. Zero dependencies, stack-agnostic.
- Status: Open Source
- URL: https://affordance.design/protocol

### IX Framework
Intelligent Experiences design methodology. A comprehensive design methodology for building AI-powered systems. Combines AI intelligence with UX design through five lenses: Intent, Interpretation, Interaction, Inference & Iteration, and Impact.
- Status: Live
- URL: https://affordance.design/ix
